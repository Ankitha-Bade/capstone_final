Effects of water scarcity

1. One in 10 people lack access to clean and
safe drinking water sources
2. Every 90 seconds a child dies
from a water-related disease
3. Globally, 1/3 of all schools lack
access to clean water and adequate sanitation
4. At any one time, 1/2 the world's hospitals beds are
occupied with patients suffering from waterborn diseases
5. Globally effects Agriculture/irrigation, economic decline,
and the earth drying out
6. The water crisis is the #1 global
risk based on impact to society
